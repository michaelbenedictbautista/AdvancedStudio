Introduction

There is widespread worry about the growing number of people that seeks medical assistance which is mostly due to population ageing, chronic illnesses, and, other related underlying disorders.To address this difficulty, the Internet of Things (IoT) appears as a promising paradigm since it offers the scalability necessary for this reason, enabling ongoing and accurate monitoring systems on a worldwide scale. I would like to proposed an IoT-based healthcare platform to allow real time monitoring for individual in mostly experiencing delicate and critical situations. I seeks to expand the infrastructure by including the use of smart wearable technology and noninvasive sensors for monitoring vital signs of a patient.
